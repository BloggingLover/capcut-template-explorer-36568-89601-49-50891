# Privacy Policy for CapCut Template Finder

**Last Updated:** November 1, 2025

## Introduction

CapCut Template Finder ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** App interactions, features used, time spent in app, crash reports
- **Internet Connection:** Network state and internet connectivity status

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do NOT collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number
- We do NOT require user registration or accounts
- We do NOT access your contacts, camera, or microphone
- We do NOT track your location

## How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain our app functionality
- Improve user experience and app performance
- Analyze app usage patterns and fix bugs
- Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
- Ensure app stability and prevent crashes

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device information and identifiers
- Ad interaction data
- General location (country/region level)

AdMob uses this data to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings.

Learn more: [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

### CapCut API
We fetch template data from CapCut's public API to display available templates. This connection does not share your personal information.

## Permissions We Request

### Required Permissions:
- **INTERNET:** To fetch template data and display content
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE:** To check internet connectivity and optimize data usage

### Why We Need These Permissions:
These permissions are essential for the app to function. Without internet access, we cannot fetch and display templates.

## Data Storage and Security

- Template data is cached locally on your device for faster loading
- Your favorite templates are stored locally on your device only
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data
- We do NOT store your data on external servers
- We do NOT share your information with third parties (except AdMob as described above)

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain age-restricted content.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View what data the app stores (locally on your device)
- **Delete:** Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
- **Opt-Out of Personalized Ads:** Use your device's ad settings to limit ad tracking

### How to Manage Ad Preferences:
- **Android:** Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS:** Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Disable Personalized Ads

## Data Retention

- Cached template data is refreshed regularly and older data is automatically removed
- Your favorites are stored until you clear app data or uninstall the app
- AdMob data retention follows Google's privacy policy
